Life is unpredictable!

Many of us know while many don't - that life is unpredictable! And among those who know, most are not able to accept it. Some accept it the way it is, but they are not able to move forward. Once you move forward, I think the cycle repeats - life again surprises us. And we don't realise when we reach the end in this roller coaster of life.

It might be good or bad, it might be simple or weird, it might scare you or not interest you at all, but expect anything to happen as life is full of surprises!

Some say that life has same amount of hardships and joys distributed differenlty for everyone. While others including me believe that it's just a myth. When Sir James Jeans proclaimed “God is a pure mathematician!” he was referring to the fact that most basic processes of nature obey elegant mathematical relationships. But let us talk about the actual processes that are too complex to be explained through mathematics. Even if there exists a solution or formula, there are infinite factors attached to it and it becomes an unsolvable problem in real time. As of now, we accept that none of the real processes in nature follows a definite pattern and there are way too many exceptions.

Elon Musk says there’s a ‘One in Billions’ chance that our reality is not a simulation. If you believe him, considering the rate of technological advancement, then a simulation must be following a pattern - and if we have enough data (of multiple simulations), we might predict what is going to happen next. But to gather enough data, we must either escape our simulations or communicate with parallel simulations (explore a new dimension). Since answers to these questions are unexplored, leaving life to be unpredictable. If this topic interests you, please follow my next article on Is our reality a simulation?

All the theories I have read on predictability of life lead to the same conclusion. Instead of trying to predict it, majority of people work in order to handle the surprises. They think that the problems are making them ready for the bigger problem, but it might be a case that there is no bigger problem or when the bigger problem comes, they will realize that they are still not strong enough. Basically what I want to say is that people who think they have mastered their attitude towards life, life can surprise them too, while on the contrary, there are people who lead a perfectly normal life and life surprises them by not giving no surprises at all!
